Requirements for a user:

1) Name (first,last) must be within allowed characters, [a-zA-Z()] and also cannot be null

2) Major cannot be null must exist

3) Year cannot be an empty string and must be a number

4) Email must end with '@ufl.edu'

5) Username must be greater than 6 characters and less than 20 characters and cannot contain a space

6) Password must be greater than 6 characters
